Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal is a famous palace in Jaipur, Rajasthan. It is also called the "Palace of Winds". The palace has many small windows and beautiful architecture. It was built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh. Built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh and designed by Lal Chand Ustad, Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds) is a five-story pink sandstone structure in Jaipur designed for royal women to observe street festivities while maintaining privacy. Its iconic 953-window honeycomb façade allows for cool breezes and intricate views. Visiting Information (2026) Operating Hours: Daily from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
