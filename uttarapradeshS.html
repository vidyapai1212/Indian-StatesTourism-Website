Uttarapradesh Tourist Places

1. Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal in Agra, India, is a 42-acre, UNESCO World Heritage site and a Wonder of the World, commissioned in 1632 by Shah Jahan as a white marble tomb for his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. It is celebrated for its perfect symmetry, pietra dura inlay, shifting colors with sunlight, and as a masterpiece of Mughal architecture.

Location: Taj Mahal,Uttarapradesh

2. Varanasi Ghats

Varanasi's ~88+ ghats are iconic stone steps lining the Ganga River, serving as the spiritual heart of the city for bathing, rituals, and, cremations. Key highlights include the, evening Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat, the, eternal, burning, pyres, at, Manikarnika, Ghat, and, peaceful, sunrise, boat, rides.

Location: Varanasi, Uttarapradesh

View Location

3. Fatehpur Sikri

Fatehpur Sikri, located near Agra, is a UNESCO World Heritage site renowned as a perfectly preserved 16th-century Mughal "ghost city" built by Emperor Akbar. It is famous for its Indo-Islamic architecture, red sandstone monuments, the massive Buland Darwaza gate, and the Tomb of Salim Chisti, which was once the capital of the Mughal Empire.

Location: Fatehpur Sikri, Uttarapradesh

View Location

4. Bara Imambara

Bara Imambara in Lucknow, built in 1784 by Nawab Asaf-ud-Daula, is world-renowned for its architectural marvels: the immense central hall—one of the world's largest unsupported, beam-free vaulted chambers—and the Bhool Bhulaiyaa, a complex, 3-story maze of 1,024 maze-like passageways designed to confuse invaders.

Location:Bara Imambara, Uttarapradesh
