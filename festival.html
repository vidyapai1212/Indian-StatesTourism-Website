Festivals in India are vibrant celebrations of cultural, seasonal, and religious diversity, strengthening community bonds and traditions. Categorized into national (Independence Day), religious (Diwali, Eid, Christmas), and harvest (Pongal, Onam) festivals, they feature unique rituals, music, and food. These celebrations promote unity and joy.
