Kabaddi

Kabaddi is a traditional Indian sport. The Pro Kabaddi League made it very popular. Kabaddi is an ancient contact team sport originating in India, specifically the state of Tamil Nadu, over 4,000 years ago. It is a unique blend of physical strength, agility, and tactical thinking that requires no specialized equipment, making it a popular sport across the Indian subcontinent and beyond.Kabaddi is a high-intensity contact team sport originating from ancient India, played between two teams of seven players on a rectangular court. The core objective is for a "raider" to enter the opposing team's half, tag as many "defenders" as possible, and return to their own half within 30 seconds while repeatedly chanting the word "kabaddi" on a single breath.
